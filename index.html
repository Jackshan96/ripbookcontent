#Why Choose RIPBOOK Flower Service#

Funerals are always emotionally very demanding on the family members. When you send a flower bouquet using a traditional florist, the chances are the family members will not even know or remember that you sent a flower.

By sending flowers via RIPBOOK, we actually display a card on the person's obituary saying that you sent a flower. This can be viewed by the family members at any time , even after many years on RIPBOOK.
